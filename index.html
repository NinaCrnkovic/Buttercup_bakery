In 2010, Buttercup Bakery opened its first location in Towson, Maryland. From its inception, Buttercup Bakery is known as bakery unique for our pink cupcakes and cakes. We take pride in using natural ingredients in our cupcakes, cakes and desserts. Our treats are as deliciously wholesome as they are beautifully decorated. We offer a homestyle bakery experience in the Baltimore/Washington Metropolitan area. Our vision is to create upscale, quick-serve bakery with a focus on simple and satisfying souther style desserts and baked goods. Our philosophy is to offer simple and delicious desserts that are easily accessible to clients via location or delivery options. Our ingredients are high quality; each dessert is carefully made with only the finest, all-natural ingredients.
